Lush Rust Studios is a collective of digital craftsmen, artists, and code poets dedicated to creating experiences, applications, and art that challenge conventional digital boundaries.

Founded in Venice Beach, we embrace the beauty of imperfection and the power of technology to inspire, soothe, and also agitate. Our work spans the spectrum from elegant software solutions to experimental digital art installations.

We don't intend to just build digital products - we cultivate digital ecosystems where technology and artistry collide.
